Pregnancy-Safe Recipes

Simple, nutritious, vegetarian recipes for each meal

Palak Paneer
Serves 2 · 30 minutes · Lunch / Dinner
VegetarianHigh ProteinIron RichCalcium
Ingredients
  • 250g fresh spinach leaves
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
Method
1
Blanch spinach for 2 mins, blend into smooth puree.
2
Heat ghee, add cumin, sauté onion until golden.
3
Add ginger-garlic paste, then tomatoes until soft.
4
Add turmeric, coriander powder, salt. Cook 2 more minutes.
5
Add spinach puree, simmer 5 mins. Fold in paneer. Finish with garam masala.
Moong Dal Khichdi with Vegetables
Serves 2 · 25 minutes · Dinner
VegetarianProteinEasy Digestion
Ingredients
  • ½ cup moong dal
  • ½ cup rice
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • ½ cup green peas
  • ½ cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ghee + spices
Method
1
Wash rice and dal. Soak 15 minutes.
2
Heat ghee in cooker, add cumin and hing.
3
Add veggies, dal, rice, spinach. Mix well.
4
Add 2.5 cups water. Cook 3 whistles.
5
Open, stir gently. Top with ghee and coriander. Serve warm.
Ragi Laddoo
Makes 12–14 · 30 minutes · Snack
VegetarianCalcium RichIronProtein
Ingredients
  • 1 cup ragi flour
  • ½ cup roasted peanuts, crushed
  • 3 tbsp jaggery powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds, roasted
  • ¼ tsp cardamom powder
Method
1
Dry roast ragi flour 8–10 minutes until fragrant.
2
Add peanuts, sesame, jaggery, cardamom.
3
Add warm ghee. Add warm water gradually until shapeable.
4
Roll into lemon-sized laddoos. Store airtight.
Haldi Doodh (Golden Milk)
Serves 1 · 5 minutes · Before Bedtime
VegetarianCalciumAnti-inflammatory
Ingredients
  • 1 glass full-fat milk
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • 3–4 strands of saffron
  • ½ tsp ghee
  • 1 tsp honey or jaggery
Method
1
Heat milk on low. Don't boil vigorously.
2
Add turmeric, pepper, saffron. Stir gently.
3
Simmer 2–3 minutes. Pepper activates curcumin.
4
Add ghee and honey. Sip slowly before sleeping.

Tips, Reminders & Cautions

Important things to keep in mind

✅ Always eat small, frequent meals

The baby is growing fast. Eat 6 small meals instead of 3 large ones. This prevents heartburn and acidity.

✅ Pair Iron-rich foods with Vitamin C

Eat iron-rich foods with Vitamin C in the same meal for double iron absorption.

✅ Soak nuts and seeds before eating

Soak almonds overnight. Soak chia and flaxseeds before use for better nutrient absorption.

✅ Steam sprouts — never eat them raw

Raw sprouts can harbour bacteria. Always lightly steam before eating.

✅ Gentle movement and sunlight

20–30 minutes morning walk for Vitamin D. Good sleep (7–8 hours) is equally vital.

⚠️ Foods to Avoid

Raw papaya, excessive pineapple, raw sprouts, unpasteurised dairy, excess spicy/oily food, excess caffeine (limit 1 cup tea/day), packaged food, excess salt, alcohol entirely.

👩‍⚕️ Always Consult Your Doctor

This guide is for general reference. Every pregnancy is unique. Always follow your gynaecologist's specific advice.
